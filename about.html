about me

"I could be bounded in a nutshell and count myself a king of infinite space" - Hamlet

I think what Hamlet was trying to convey is that although humans are rather limited physically, compared to the vast nature of the universe, our imagination is free to explore anywhere we want. Einstein declared that nothing could travel faster than the speed of light when he set forth his theory of relativity. Such imprudence! I dare say my mind can travel faster than the ultimate cosmic speed limit as I dive past the event horizons of supermassive black holes and even freeze time as and when I deem fit while I attempt to comprehend quantum mechanics.

As naive as it may sound, I think mankind should come together as one human race and set aside our differences so that we could fund more advanced deep space exploration and scientific research in order to expand our boundaries, instead of splurging on self-destructive weapons.
